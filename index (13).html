The Band
Who We Are

The Coyote Stylite is a Texas southern rock band with a touch of soul — born in the dirt and built for the stage. Fronted by Jimmy Zapata, a big-voiced Texas soul singer compared to Chris Stapleton, Jamey Johnson, and Bon Scott of AC/DC, this band takes audiences on a full ride.

High-energy rockers. Country heart. Grunge edge. Classic rock grit. They weave it all together in a live set that keeps a crowd singing from the first note to last call. Original music meets an arsenal of covers that play the room like a fine-tuned instrument.

From the dancehalls of Cleburne to the brewery stages of Waxahachie — wherever The Coyote Stylite sets up, they own the night.
